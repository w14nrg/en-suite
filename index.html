About En-Suite Bathrooms

Specialist En-Suite Bathroom Installers with Decades of Experience

En-Suite Bathrooms focuses solely on one thing: creating well-designed, reliable en-suite bathrooms that genuinely add comfort and value. Drawing on over 25 years’ experience in bathrooms, plumbing and property refurbishment, we handle everything from initial ideas to the final clean down.

Recent UK property research suggests that adding an extra bathroom can typically increase a home’s value by around 4–8% and boost rental income by roughly 6%, depending on location and specification. A well-planned en-suite can also make a property easier to sell and more attractive to tenants and guests.
