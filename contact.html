Book Your Free En-Suite Survey & 3D Design

Whether you’re adding an en-suite to your own bedroom, converting a box room, or planning multiple en-suites for a rental or HMO, the first step is a quick conversation and a free home survey.

During the survey we’ll:

  • Measure the space and check drainage, ventilation and water pressure.
  • Talk through how you want to use the room – everyday practicality vs. hotel feel.
  • Advise on what’s realistic and what will give you the best value.
  • Arrange a free 3D design so you can see the layout before committing.

You can call us on 0207 371 3333, email info@en-suite.co.uk or use the form on this page.
