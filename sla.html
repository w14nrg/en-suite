Landlord & HMO Packages

En-Suite Bathroom Packages for Rentals, HMOs & Co-Living

Designed around yield, durability and compliance – not just looks.
Whether you own a single rental, a small HMO or a larger co-living property, our fixed-structure en-suite packages make it simple to add or upgrade bathrooms that tenants actually value and that stand up to daily use.

Many UK market reports suggest that an extra bathroom can typically add around 4–8% to a property's value and help command a higher rent – often particularly noticeable in shared houses and HMOs. We build en-suites that are robust, easy to clean and straightforward to maintain, so you see the benefit long after the work is finished.

What’s Included in a Landlord En-Suite Package?

A package from En-Suite Bathrooms is more than just a quote. It’s a structured way of delivering the same things landlords and HMO owners ask us for again and again: predictable costs, durable finishes and minimal disruption to tenants.

  • Free property survey – technical assessment of drainage routes, ventilation, water pressure and electrics.
  • Free 3D design – layout and visuals tailored to rental use, including storage, lighting and wipe-clean finishes.
  • Fixed-structure package – clear scope of work so you know exactly what’s included in each option.
  • Professional installation – plumbing, electrics, tiling, waterproofing and finishing managed by one team.
  • Documentation – where required, we can provide certificates for electrical and relevant works.

All packages are priced after survey, based on the actual property, access and specification – but the structure and what’s included remains the same, so it’s easy to compare options.

Choose the Right En-Suite Package for Your Property

Essential Landlord En-Suite

Best for: Single lets and standard rentals adding their first en-suite.

  • Free survey & 3D layout for one en-suite.
  • Durable, mid-range sanitaryware (toilet, basin, shower).
  • Practical tiling or waterproof wall panels in wet areas.
  • Water-saving fittings to help control usage.
  • Flooring chosen for grip and easy cleaning.

Ideal if you want a straightforward, reliable en-suite that tenants appreciate without over-specifying the finish.

Yield Booster (Small HMO)

Best for: Shared houses and HMOs typically up to 5–6 bedrooms.

  • Free survey & 3D planning for multiple en-suites or shared layouts.
  • Hard-wearing brassware with simple, robust controls.
  • High-impact, wipe-clean finishes for walls and floors.
  • Smart storage and lighting to maximise compact spaces.
  • Specification tuned to target rent and local market.

Ideal if you want to improve room desirability, reduce bathroom queues and justify higher rents in a shared property.

Premium HMO & Co-Living

Best for: Higher-end HMOs, co-living spaces and guest accommodation.

  • Free survey & 3D concept for a full property or floor.
  • Upgraded finishes – feature tiling, niches, better lighting.
  • Options for walk-in showers, glass screens and designer brassware.
  • Designs aimed at boosting reviews and long-term occupancy.
  • Phased installation planning to keep rooms earning where possible.

Ideal if your strategy is premium co-living or boutique-style HMOs, where presentation and durability both matter.

Package & Maintenance Notes

  • Scope: Packages cover the design and installation of new or upgraded en-suite bathrooms. Structural alterations, major rerouting of services and unrelated building works may be quoted separately if required.
  • Property Condition: Quotations assume the property is in a reasonable state of repair. Pre-existing issues (e.g. hidden leaks, rotten timbers, non-compliant electrics) will be highlighted and may require additional remedial work.
  • Compliance: All work is carried out with regard to current building regulations and good practice for plumbing, waterproofing and electrics. Where necessary we can work alongside building control or your own professional team.
  • Maintenance Plans: Maintenance options are designed for en-suites installed by En-Suite Bathrooms. Full details and pricing are confirmed after survey and depend on property size, number of en-suites and location.
  • Pricing: All pricing is confirmed in writing after the free survey and 3D design stage. No work is carried out until you have approved the final scope and quotation.
  • Full Details: For full terms and example documentation, please email info@en-suite.co.uk.
