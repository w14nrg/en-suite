About En-Suite Bathrooms

From General Plumbing to a Dedicated En-Suite Specialist

En-Suite Bathrooms was born out of decades of hands-on experience in plumbing, bathrooms and property renovation. After years of working on every part of a home – from boilers and pipework to full refurbishments – we noticed one thing: the projects that made the biggest difference to how people lived, and to what their homes were worth, were almost always the additional bathrooms and en-suites.

That’s why we chose to specialise. Today, instead of trying to be “all trades to all people”, En-Suite Bathrooms focuses on one clear goal: designing and installing beautiful, space-efficient en-suite bathrooms that add comfort, privacy and value for homeowners, landlords, HMOs and small accommodation providers.

Drawing on a family history in the building and plumbing trade, we’ve refined our process so that every project starts with a proper technical survey and ends with a finished en-suite that looks like the 3D design you were first shown. We understand drainage routes, ventilation, water pressure, structural issues and building regulations – but we also understand how people actually live in the spaces we create.

Why We Focus on En-Suites

A well-planned en-suite does far more than just add a shower and toilet. It can:

  • Turn a standard bedroom into a true master suite.
  • Make shared houses, HMOs and rentals more attractive and easier to let.
  • Reduce morning queues and friction in busy family homes.
  • Unlock “dead” space – such as box rooms, alcoves, landings and loft corners – and turn it into something genuinely useful.
  • Help increase both property value and rental income, depending on location and specification.

Recent UK property research suggests that adding an extra bathroom can typically increase a home’s value by around 4–8% and boost rental income by roughly 6%, particularly in urban areas and shared households. While exact figures always depend on area and finish, one thing is clear: a thoughtful en-suite is one of the most cost-effective improvements many owners can make.

How We Work

Every En-Suite Bathrooms project follows a clear, simple process:

Free Home Survey – We visit the property, assess the space, check pipe routes, soil stacks, ventilation and electrics, and discuss how you want to use the room.

Free 3D Design – You receive a detailed 3D layout showing different options for shower, basin and toilet positions, plus storage, tiling and lighting ideas.

Fixed-Price Quotation – We give you a clear written quote with a defined scope of works, so you know exactly what’s included and what your new en-suite will cost.

Professional Installation – Our team handles everything: plumbing, electrics, carpentry, tiling, waterproofing, decoration and final testing, keeping disruption to a minimum.

Aftercare & Guarantees – We stand behind our workmanship and are on hand if you ever have questions after the job is complete.
